What am I looking at?

Publications most similar to methods based on abstracts

How similar are your publications to a large set based on the context? I first derived a vector representation for each of the methods in the large set using a method called word2vec. I could then parse the text from each of your publication abstracts, and map it to this space by taking a mean vector for all the words I find in your abstract. I can then calculate a similarity score of this vector to all the method vectors individually. What you are looking at is a list of top matches (scores > 0) that are matched to {{ themethod }}
